About Gracious Angeli Travel & Tours

Our Story

Gracious Angeli' Travel and Tours is a company with great potential for higher growth and development. Founded in June 1999 and having achieved high standards of development in the tourism industry, it can truly be described as one of the leading travel agencies in the Luzon area. Ms. Judith Balisong Balabag, the general manager and owner of the company, was in charge of the operation for twenty-six (26) years. With these 26 years, she has garnered a lot of working experience to enable her to succeed in her endeavor. When the pandemic ravaged the globe, she has been working on her own and has been able to penetrate into certain fora and platform in this business which she was never aware of, and which are great for this company now.

Mission

Gracious Angeli' Travel & Tours is committed to providing our clients with affordable and transparent travel experiences, while ensuring that every detail of their itinerary is managed with utmost care. We strongly believe that travel should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget, and we strive to create itineraries that are both exciting and cost effective.

Vision

We aim to produce world class travel consultants and become a preferred travel service provider.

Objective

The major objective of GRACIOUS ANGELI TRAVEL AND TOURS is to provide Domestic and International Air Ticketing, Local and International Packaged Tours, Visa Assistance and other ancillary tourism-related services to the members of the public.

The Market

Our target market is the general public, which includes the public and private sector. Corporate booking is very essential for the business to survive. Therefore, our target market are as follows:

  • •Corporate (Private Companies) sector
  • •The Public Sector/Government Agencies
  • •Civic Organizations
  • •Religious Organizations
  • •Local NGO's
  • •Families/Individuals
  • •Social and Professional Organizations

Our Partners

Department of Tourism Accreditation

Government-Recognized Travel Agency

Gracious Angeli Travel & Tours is officially accredited by the Department of Tourism (DOT), Philippines. Our DOT accreditation ensures you are booking with a trusted, government-recognized travel agency committed to quality and safety.

"Know all men by these present that Gracious Angeli Travel & Tours with address at Level 2 Robinson Place, Maharlika Highway, Brgy. Mabini, Santiago City having complied with the requirements prescribed by the Department of Tourism in hereby granted regular accreditation as a travel and tour agency pursuant to the provisions of Republic Act No. 9593 and rules regulations promulgated by the Department of Tourism to implement the intent and purpose of the said Republic Act."

Our Partners

We proudly collaborate with reputable organizations to provide you with trusted and quality travel services.

NITAS

Network of Independent Travel and Allied Services

Network of Independent Travel and Allied Services, Philippines, Inc. or NITAS for short was established in 1986. It is a business travel association who's 400 members are small to medium to medium high companies scattered all over the country.

TPBPHL

Tourism Promotions Board Philippines

An attached agency of the Department of Tourism, the Tourism Promotions Board (TPB) exists to market and promote the Philippines domestically and internationally as a world-class tourism and MICE destination, in strategic partnership with private and public stakeholders to deliver a unique high-value experience for visitors, significantly contributing to increased arrivals, receipts and investments to the country.

LOVE THE PHILIPPINES

Department of Tourism Campaign

The new tourism campaign slogan of the Department of Tourism. The "enhanced" tourism campaign slogan, launched by the department during its 50th-anniversary celebration at the Manila Hotel on Tuesday (June 27, 2023), replaces the 11-year-old It's More Fun in the Philippines.
