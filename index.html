About Gracious Angeli Travel & Tours

Our Story

Gracious Angeli' Travel and Tours is a company with great potential for higher growth and development. Founded in June 1999 and having achieved high standards of development in the tourism industry, it can truly be described as one of the leading travel agencies in the Luzon area. Ms. Judith Balisong Balabag, the general manager and owner of the company, was in charge of the operation for twenty-four (26) years. With these 24 years, she has garnered a lot of working experience to enable her to succeed in her endeavor. When the pandemic ravaged the globe, she has been working on her own and has been able to penetrate into certain fora and platform in this business which she was never aware of, and which are great for this company now.

Mission

Gracious Angeli' Travel & Tours is committed to providing our clients with affordable and transparent travel experiences, while ensuring that every detail of their itinerary is managed with utmost care. We strongly believe that travel should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget, and we strive to create itineraries that are both exciting and cost effective.

Vision

We aim to produce world class travel consultants and become a preferred travel service provider.

Objective

The major objective of GRACIOUS ANGELI TRAVEL AND TOURS is to provide Domestic and International Air Ticketing, Local and International Packaged Tours, Visa Assistance and other ancillary tourism-related services to the members of the public.
